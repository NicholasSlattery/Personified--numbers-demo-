How to Play

  1. Player 1 spins for a secret number.
  2. Player 1 gives clues as if the number were a person (e.g., "What color are its eyes?").
  3. Player 2 asks questions and guesses.
  4. Press Correct on a right guess, or Incorrect Guess for a miss.
  5. Roles swap. A full round is when both players have had a turn describing.
  6. Winner is the player with the most points. Ties are broken by fewest incorrect guesses.
